Privacy Policy

Welcome to Our app This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

the app is designed to help you discover and track movies and TV shows. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly:
    • Search Queries: When you search for movies or TV shows, we process your search terms to fetch results.
    • Watchlist Information: If you use the watchlist feature, we store information about the movies and TV shows you add, mark as watched, or remove. This data is stored locally on your device and may be synced if cloud backup features are implemented.
    • Preferences: We may store your preferences, such as theme settings (light/dark mode), selected language, and region for content display. These are typically stored locally on your device.
  • Information collected automatically:
    • Usage Data: We may collect general, non-personally identifiable information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and session duration, to improve app performance and user experience. This data is anonymized.
    • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device (e.g., operating system version) for troubleshooting and to optimize the app for your device. This does not include unique device identifiers that can personally identify you without your consent.
  • Information from Third-Party Services:
    • TMDB API: the app uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch movie and TV show information, including details, cast, ratings, images, and watch provider information. Our interaction with the TMDB API is governed by TMDB's own API terms of service and privacy policy. We do not send your personal watchlist data or specific user account information to TMDB, other than what is necessary to make API requests (e.g., region preferences for watch providers).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality, such as searching for content, displaying details, and managing your watchlist.
  • To personalize your experience, for example, by displaying content relevant to your selected region or language.
  • To improve the app, including bug fixing, performance enhancements, and developing new features.

CineScope does not provide any content streaming services. We help you track and discover content.
