Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I add a movie/TV show to my watchlist?

A: Navigate to the details page of the movie or TV show you're interested in. You'll find a bookmark icon (often a heart or ribbon). Tap this icon to add the item to your watchlist. Tap it again to remove it.

Q: Can I stream movies or TV shows directly in CineScope?

A: No, CineScope does not provide streaming services. Our app helps you discover, track, and find information about where to watch content on various third-party platforms. We provide links to TMDB for watch provider information when available.

Q: The information for a movie/TV show seems incorrect or is missing. What can I do?

A: CineScope uses The Movie Database (TMDB) as its primary source for all content information. TMDB is a community-driven database. If you find inaccuracies, you can contribute to TMDB directly to help improve the data for everyone. You can visit themoviedb.org to learn more about contributing.

Q: How do I change the app's theme or region settings?

A: You can find options to change the theme (e.g., light/dark mode) and your preferred content region in the app's Settings screen.

Q: I can't find a specific movie/TV show.

A: Ensure your search query is spelled correctly. Our search is powered by TMDB. If a title is very new or obscure, it might not be in the database yet, or your search terms might be too broad or too narrow. Try different keywords or check TMDB directly.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter a bug or have a technical problem with the app, please help us by providing as much detail as possible:

  • What version of CineScope are you using? (You can find this in the Settings screen)
  • What is your device model and operating system version?
  • What steps did you take before the issue occurred?
  • If possible, include screenshots or a screen recording of the issue.

You can send this information to our support email address listed below.
